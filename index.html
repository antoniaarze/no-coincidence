About the Series

A blog about the reasons underneath.

Almost nothing you buy, follow, or love is random. There's always a reason, a nudge, a story, a feeling someone engineered. NO COINCIDENCE digs into the why: the psychology, the marketing, the patterns we don't stop to question. Each issue picks one thing people are obsessed with and asks the same quiet question. Why?
